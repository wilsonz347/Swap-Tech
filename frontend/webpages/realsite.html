Our Mission

let’s take control of our purchasing power!

Technology is always developing for consumers to purchase newer, faster, and ‘better’ devices. But at what cost are we innovating? Every year, we mine 2.8 billion tonnes of raw metals, causing 4-7% of global greenhouse gas emissions. In addition to displacing, destroying, and exploiting communities in the global south. We aim to combat this issue by taking back our purchasing power from large corporations and incentivising ethical technical consumption.
